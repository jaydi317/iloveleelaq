3 Experiments Anyone Can Run at Home
No lab required. Just curiosity and a willingness to be surprised.
01
Seed Germination
The simplest controlled experiment. Seeds don't have beliefs, expectations, or placebo responses. They either grow — or they don't.
  1. Get two paper towels and dampen both equally
  2. Place identical seeds on each (same packet, same count)
  3. Charge water in the Bloc for one group
  4. Water both daily with their respective water
  5. Photograph daily. Compare at Day 5
Community average: charged seeds sprout 1–3 days faster
02
Flower Longevity
Buy two identical flowers from the same bunch. One gets regular water. One gets Bloc-charged water. Watch what happens over 5 days.
  1. Buy two identical cut flowers from the same bunch
  2. Place in identical vases with equal water
  3. Charge one vase's water in the Bloc for 4 minutes
  4. Keep both in the same location, same light
  5. Photograph daily. Compare at Day 5
Community reports: charged flowers lasting 2–5 days longer on average
03
Water Taste Test
The quickest experiment. Takes 5 minutes. Bring a friend — do it blind. The water speaks for itself.
  1. Fill two identical glasses with the same water
  2. Charge one glass in the Bloc for 4 minutes
  3. Have someone else label them A and B (you don't look)
  4. Taste both. Note which feels smoother, softer
  5. Reveal which was charged
Philipp says: "About 80% of people can tell the difference immediately"
